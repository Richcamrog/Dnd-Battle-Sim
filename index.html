D&D 5e Battle Simulator

Runs 1000 Monte Carlo combats per map to predict how your party fares. Single HTML file — works offline after first load, shareable via email or Discord.

Monster Management

  • ⚡ Auto-fill — Populates encounter with monsters matching the terrain type at current difficulty XP budget
  • Search box — Type any monster name to search the full 5etools bestiary, click to add
  • + Custom — Opens a form to manually enter any monster with custom stats (use this if search doesn't find what you want)
  • × buttons — Remove individual monsters. −/+ change count. Clear wipes the whole map.
  • Any monster from 5etools Monster Manual 2024 (XMM) will appear in search once bestiary loads

Terrain Rules

  • Open Field: No modifiers
  • Dungeon Corridor: All combatants +2 AC (half cover). All ranged attacks at disadvantage (2d20 take lower).
  • Forest: Each attack has 50% chance of +5 AC cover. Monsters in difficult terrain lose roughly 25% of extra attacks.

Combat Rules

  • Standard initiative (d20 + DEX mod). Natural 20 = double damage dice (critical hit).
  • Death saves at 0 HP: 3 successes = stable, 3 failures = dead. Nat 1 = 2 failures. Nat 20 = revive at 1 HP.
  • Monster AI: focus fire on highest-threat PC (casters first), coordinate all monsters on same target.
  • Max 20 rounds; surviving conscious PCs = win.
